Services
Here at The Computer Shack we specialize in computer related tasks.
That does not mean however, that we will not accept other tech related requests.
If you have a non computer tech Problem then feel free to ask me about it
and if I do not know how to handle it myself I can guarantee that I know someone who does.

Prices
The main services offered here involve computer repairs, computer upgrades, and new computer builds. This applies to desktops and laptop however prices vary based upon time costs and hardware.
For computer builds I will do my best to find the cheapest components that you request or that fit your specifcations to keep costs low, and the cost of parts will then be added to your total. In addition to this there will be an added $150 for the build of single motherboard machines and $300 for multi motherboard server builds.
For computer upgrades The cost is the cost of the upgrade along with $50 per components ie: new motherboard $50, new gpu $50 etc
Lastly repair jobs are more tricky and require time spent analyzing the problem before a decision can be made. So I will inspect the hardware free of charge, tell you the recommended repairs it needs and then offer my price to do so. I am open to negotiation and if my prices are too steep then there will be no hard feelings if you go somewhere else.
Any other tech related jobs can be discussed and if I have the ability to provide what you need then prices can be determined and haggled.
